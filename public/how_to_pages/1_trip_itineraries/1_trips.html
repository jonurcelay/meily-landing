1. Trip Itineraries

The main feature of Meily is to let you organize trip itineraries in a single interface.

Create Trip Itineraries

Trips can be easily generated with AI, from imported social media posts, or directly from scratch (adding stops manually as desired). The social media posts from which posts can be scanned are Instagram, TikTok, and Youtube.

Modify and Use your Trip Itinerary

Once the trip is created, you can further modify it by changing the order of days/stops, and by adding/deleting them, which can be assisted by AI or by social media posts.
