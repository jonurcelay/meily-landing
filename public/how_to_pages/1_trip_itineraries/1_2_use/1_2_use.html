1.2 How to use a trip itinerary

Once you have created your trip itinerary, as shown in 1.1. How to Create Trip Itineraries, you will be able to edit it and use it. This section shows how you can add/remove/reorder days and stops from your trip, how you can save your trip, and finally, how you can remove old trips. Ready? Let's get started

Edit and save

Once you are inside your trip itinerary page, notice that the background will be either blue or white (see images below).

If the background is blue, it means that you are in Edit Mode, so that you can modify the days and stops of the trip (shown in the next subsection). When you are in edit mode, the buttons at the top rigth of the screen allow you to discard/save the changes for your trip itinerary.

If the background is white, it means that you are not in edit mode, so you cannot edit the itinerary. In this case, the button at the top right of the screen enables you to enter edit mode.

Add new days

Once you are in edit mode, you can add new days to your trip.

For this, you just need to tap in the + Add day button at the bottom of the "Overview" page of the trip itinerary. The next three options will be shown:

  • ✓ Add day with AI
  • ✓ Scan from posts
  • ✓ Create empty day

Select the + Add day button

Three options for adding a new day

✓ Adding days with AI

If choosing this option, Meily will analyze your current trip itinerary, looking at your preferences, dates, and planned days/stops. Based on that, it will generate the stops that best fit for an additional day in your current plan, avoiding already visited destinations. Once it finishes loading you will be able to see the recommended stops for the new day.

Add new stops

If you are edit mode, you can also add new stops to the day of your trip itinerary.

For this, you just need to tap in the + Add stop button at the bottom of the day page. The next three options will be shown:

  • ✓ Add stop with AI
  • ✓ Scan from posts
  • ✓ Pick stop myself

Select the + Add stop button

Three options for adding a new stop

✓ Add stop with AI

If choosing this option, Meily will analyze your current trip itinerary, looking at your preferences, dates, and planned days/stops. Based on that, it will generate the next stops that best fits your day, avoiding already existing stops in your plan. Once it finishes loading you will be able to see the recommended stop.

Reorder and delete days/stops

Finally, you can can also reorder and delete the days/stops of your trip itinerary, so you can organize yourself better.

For reordering days and stops, drag them up and down through the itinerary. To delete them, swipe them to the left, and the confirm the deletion.

Remember that for this you need to be in edit mode. If for some reason you want to discard the change you have made to the trip and recover your last itinerary version, you can discard changes by clickin in the x in the top-right corner of the screen.
