1.1 How to create trip itineraries

On this page you will find a step by step guide showing how to create a trip itinerary with the different options that Meily Trips offers:

  • ✓ Automatically with AI, getting a recommended trip itinerary
  • ✓ From imported social media posts, getting a trip itinerary based on the stops mentioned on the chosen posts
  • ✓ Adding stops manually, starting from an empty template

As an example, a trip to San Francisco will be created, showing all the steps and options in the process. For easier understanding, screenshots of the app will be included for all the steps. Let's get started!

First Steps: Choose Trip Options

Once in the Trips page, tab on "+" to generate a new trip. You will be asked to enter your travel destination, preferences, dates for the trip, and the transport for the trip. The images below show how these options look on the screen.
