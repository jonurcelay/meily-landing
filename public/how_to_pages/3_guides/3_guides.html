3. Guides

The main feature of Meily is to let you organize trip itineraries, as seen in section 1. Trip Itineraries. You can also scan posts from social media, as explained in section 2. Posts. But there is one more thing you can do with Meily Trips, which is to create AI-powered local tourist guides that will answer your questions and explain what you are seeing during your trip. This section explains how to create and use these custom tourist guides.

Create guides

Guides can be easily created in the app by selecting the custom guide generation options. You can also create new guides and link them to existing trip itineraries.

Using the guides

Once the guides are created and saved into the app, you can use them by sending them questions or images. They will provide you all the information you need, as each guide is a local expert at the chosen destination.
