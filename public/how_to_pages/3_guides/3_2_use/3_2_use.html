3.2 How to use a guide

Hey! This is the last section of How to Use Meily trips. Nice to know that you have made it this far in the documentation! Let us know if you found the documentation useful and if there is something that is not clear, we will be happy to review it and improve it!

That being said, once you have created your guides, as shown in 3.1. How to create a guide, you will be asking yourself how to use it. This is exactly what this section explains step by step. You will be able to ask questions and send images to your local guides, so they will answer with local insights, recommendations, and information. Ready for seeing exactly how to do it? Let's get started!

Send images

Once you open your guide, you will be able to send images and get audio answers from your guide. For example, you can send an image of a monument, and your local tourist guide will provide a clear description of what you are seeing.

Sending images will not be activated if you are not located close to the guide's destination, as the knowledge of the guide is specific for his region.

Guide memory

Once you are in the guide page, and have started a conversation with your guide, you will be able to access all the sent images/questions and audio answers from your guide. For this, click on the top-right button with the picture of the guide, and the guide memory window will be opened.

The images below show how to access the memory of a guide:

Deleting a guide

Finally, if you want to delete one of your guides, you just need to go to the Guides page, and swipe to the left the guide you want to delete. Once you do this, a new window will popup to confirm the deletion of the chosen guide.
