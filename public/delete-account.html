Delete Account

To delete your Meily Trips account and associated data:

  1. Send an email to support@meily.app with the subject "DELETE ACCOUNT".
  2. This email MUST be sent from the same email that holds the account to be deleted.
  3. In the content of the email, write the reason for deleting the account.

All your data will be permanently removed. You will receive a confirmation email.
